# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
*   **Formal and Authoritative:** Emphasizes accuracy and precision, suitable for a serious genealogical research tool.

## Visual Identity and Branding
*   **Clean and Minimalist Design:** Focus on usability and readability, reducing visual clutter.

## User Interaction and Experience
*   **Efficiency and Speed:** Optimize workflows for quick data entry and navigation, especially for experienced users.

## Community and Support
*   **Active User Forums and Online Communities:** Encourage peer-to-peer support and knowledge sharing.

## Core Values
*   **Open Source and Community-Driven:** Upholding principles of transparency, collaboration, and free access.
*   **Innovation and Continuous Improvement:** Regularly introducing new features and enhancing existing ones based on user feedback and technological advancements.
*   **Data Integrity and Accuracy:** Prioritizing the reliability and correctness of genealogical data.
*   **User Empowerment:** Providing tools that give users control over their data and research process.

# Initial Concept

Gramps is a genealogy program designed for both hobbyists and professional genealogists, offering intuitive usage and comprehensive features.

# Product Guide

## Target Users
*   Hobbyist genealogists looking for an intuitive way to organize their family history.
*   Professional genealogists requiring feature-rich tools for in-depth research and analysis.
*   Researchers and historians interested in data visualization and historical record keeping.

## Core Goals and Objectives
*   Provide robust data management for genealogical information.
*   Offer intuitive and user-friendly interfaces for data entry and visualization.
*   Support advanced analytical tools for complex genealogical research.
*   Facilitate collaboration among genealogists.
*   Ensure data privacy and security for sensitive family information.
